Study smarter. Not harder.

Study Scheduler allows the creation of a stress-free plan to work towards learning effectively.

Take it easy.

Studying can be stressful. When you have a large pile of things to get through, it sometimes feels like it's impossible to get out. What to do first? What next? Study Schedule hopes to solve these problems. By planning out your strategy to tackle the task at hand, you will be one step ahead.

Up in the cloud.

Busy life, busy schedule. In our day an age, a majority of people find themselves out and about. This means that the devices at hand switch and swap around frequently. Study Scheduler has taken this to account and simply saves your schedule to the cloud. Access, modify, and stay organized no matter where you are.

Designed by students.

Study Scheduler is coded, designed, and developed by students. With this in mind, the platform has been crafted to suit and gear towards students alike.
